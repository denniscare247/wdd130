The Oba of bini

The Oba of Benin is a very powerful king in Nigeria. You cannot talk about

dynasty without talking about the kingship and stool. The King of Oba

is a force in Nigeria who is the traditional ruler of the Eweka dynasty

that is legendary. He is regarded as the number one man who stands

between the gods and man in the kingdom. His words are powerful and

his decisions are believe to be finally. This may be the modern

world but he still performs some ancient practices that would

amaze you.

Bini Bronze Work

here is no doubt that the Bini Kingdom has made its name in brass casting.

The Bini art ranges from Ebony wood, terracotta, brass, bronze and ivory.

The bronze of this people depicts a show of different things that you can

identify with.

Bini Dancing Step

The way the people display their dancing skills is magnificent. There is

nowhere in the world where you find this people displaying their dance steps

that you would not recognize them. They are unique and exceptionally great in

showcasing their culture to the world.
